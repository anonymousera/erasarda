Resource Gallery

  • TOEFL Prep (105/120): LinkedIn post- It follows the old TOEFL pattern, but the resources given are still very useful. Also do utilize all the free material available on ets website for the prep. They also provide around 20 free sample writing questions with scoring. Also try to schedule your toefl only a few days or weeks after your GRE. The practice of GRE helps here too.
  • GRE Prep (327/340): All official material provided by ETS.
    For Vocab section practice is the key. There always will be a limit on the number of words you can remember. 500-800 words should be sufficient. Use smart-work in this case. Choose the most relevant ones. I used Barron’s 333 High Frequency Words. Revise daily. Note down the words you didn’t know and which came up during practice, especially those from official resources like official GRE Verbal Reasoning Practice Questions book. Use elimination technique. The options can be very confusing many times, even when you know the word meanings. Therefore, first give your best guess to the blanks, then only see the options. DO NOT use the reverse technique.
